History of KLE Society:


K.L..E. Society's SSMS BCA College born to build future Technician. College is located in the outskirts of the town. The surrounding cool and fantastic whether away from pollution brings the study atmosphere among the students, the prime focus on Innovative and Creative teaching-learning methodologies. Skill learning by adding job-oriented and job-linked inputs. Regularly updating the pedagogical skills of the teachers and administrators by conducting in service training programmes, workshops etc. It is therefore the aim of the institution to develop every innate trait of the students the physical, the intellectual and spiritual, to meet challenges and needs of careers, individual development and dizzying ambitions.






About SSMS College:


The college has well equipped building and we have spacious classrooms, modernized computer labs with wifi facility

Athani is border place of Karnataka and Educationally backward region in Management education so our society is decided to start Computer Science course BCA to impart potential Education in Computer Science to the Rural area of Athani. Because it is very close to Maharastra a border area of Maharastra is enriched with the Industries and there is requirement of potential Computer Science people observing such type of opportunities our society intended to produce upcoming Computer Science human resources. And Principal Shree Shivayogi Muraghendra Swamiji College Athani Shri. M.V.Gadad started with zeal and there sincere efforts to establish the BCA course in the year 2007.
